FAQ's

What products does Pharma24*7 offer?

Pharma24*7 offers three products

  • Pharmacy Software: For medical stores to manage billing, inventory, and GST.
  • Chemist App: For medical stores to manage billing, inventory, and GST.
  • Patient App: For customers to order medicines online and get digital prescriptions.

these three products be used together?

Yes! The Pharmacy Software, Chemist App, and Patient App are designed to work together seamlessly.

Does Pharma24*7 work for both small and large pharmacies?

Yes, it is suitable for single stores, multi-store chains, and large pharmacies.

Is Pharma24*7 available offline?

No, Pharma24*7 is a cloud-based software and requires an active internet connection to function.

Is Pharma24*7 available on all devices?

Yes, all three products are available on PC, tablets, and mobile phones.

What is Pharma24*7 Pharmacy Software used for?

It helps medical stores manage billing, inventory, GST filing, customer records, and more.

Can I generate GST invoices?

Yes, Pharma24*7 provides automatic GST billing with tax reports.

Does it support barcode scanning?

Yes, you can use barcode scanning for fast and accurate billing.

Can I track expired medicines?

Yes, the software alerts you about upcoming medicine expiries.

Does it have supplier payment tracking?

Yes, you can track payments, outstanding balances, and supplier accounts.

Can I send invoices via WhatsApp?

yes, you can send digital invoices and payment reminders via WhatsApp.

What is the Pharma24*7 Chemist App?

It's a mobile app for pharmacists to manage orders, customers, and business operations.

Can I receive medicine orders from customers?

Yes, customers can place orders via the Patient App, and you'll get notifications.

Does the Chemist App support medicine stock updates?

Yes, you can add, edit, and update medicine stock directly in the app.

Can I offer home delivery to customers?

Yes, you can enable home delivery services through the Chemist App.

Can I accept digital payments in the app?

Yes, you can accept UPI, credit/debit cards, and net banking payments.

What is the Pharma24*7 Patient App?

It's a mobile app for customers to order medicines, upload prescriptions, and track orders.

Can customers upload prescriptions?

Yes, customers can upload prescriptions, and you can verify them before dispensing medicines.

Can customers track their medicine orders?

Yes, real-time order tracking is available in the app.

Can customers receive reminders for medicine refills?

Yes, the app sends reminders for medicine refills based on past orders.
